The black body is a body that absorbs all the EM waves that fall on it. Inside, there is no escape for the light, so it just stays there. 'As the heat of the Sun travels from Sun to Earth with the speed of light, it reaches Earth in 8,33 minutes. Both heat and light are electromagnetic waves. They, however, have different wavelengths and frequencies. Visible light has higher frequencies than the heat (infrared light). Besides visible light and infrared radiation, the Sun emits as well ultraviolet radiation and other electromagnetic waves.
